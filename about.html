Anish Forge PVT. LTD.

Our unit is run by qualified technocrats with strong work experience in the forging industry. The company is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company. We have full flagged forging and machining shop. Following are the brief details about various facilities in our company.

Forging:

We have 3 (three) nos. belt drop hammers of 1 (one) ton capacity with all supporting machinery like Billet Heaters. We also have three upstroke type friction screw presses (200 Ton, 400 Ton & 600 ton capacity). We have one 1000 ton forging press,We can produce forging ranging from 0.5 to 10 kg. We have installed capacity of processing 450 MT every month.

Machining

From January 2005 we have started machining unit . There we have CNC machining facility. We have four CNC turning machines. The details of the machines are attached herewith. We have in house semi finish machining facility. In JAN 2011 we have started a full flagged Normalising heat treatment shop.We are also going to increase our CNC machining facility.
